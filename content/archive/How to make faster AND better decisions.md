# How to make faster AND better decisions?
Faster decisions are risky. They expose you to more uncertainty.
On the other side slower decisions reduce your ability to catch opportunities.
To be faster AND better at this first of all you need principles.
At that point:
* delegate the whole problem to somebody more competent than you,
* or give yourself a time constraint (5 seconds, 2 minutes, or whatever).
