#   Why an ETFs index instead of a Mutual Index Fund?
If you plan to invest long term, the difference between the two are not that big.
That said ETFs:
* are generally cheaper than mutual index funds, if bought commission-free,
* have often lower minimum investments,
* are more tax-efficient.
