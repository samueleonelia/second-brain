# Basic rule for the main points of an article online
More main points = Less explanations 
Less main points = More explanations
More and less here are a function of how long the article is.
For a piece of 800-1200 words, 3 main points will require a longer explanation to fill the space. In a 200 words piece, the same 3 points will be much shorter.
