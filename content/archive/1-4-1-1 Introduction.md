# 1-4-1-1 Introduction
The role of each sentence:
1. opener,

__
2. clarifies the opener,
3. reinforces your point with a credibility statement,
4. rounds out the argument,
5. speaks to the emotional benefit of the reader

__
5. conclusion,

__
6. why this conclusion matters.
