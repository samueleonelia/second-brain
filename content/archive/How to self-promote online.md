# How to self-promote online
Put the reader at the center of your writing.
Use your personal experience as a reference to give context, reliability or authority to what you say.
This way you can use the *Golden Intersection*:
* tell a story,
* relevant for the reader problems,
* with valuable information about yourself or your product (as context, examples or proof of credibility).
