# How does a lead magnet work
1. You create a landing page with a form to subscribe to your mailing list.
2. You connect it to your autoresponder.
3. The copy of the landing page promises a lead magnet: *“Subscribe and you will receive this free ebook.”*
4. You set up an automation in the autoresponder. When visitors complete the subscription process, they will receive straight away the promised lead magnet.
