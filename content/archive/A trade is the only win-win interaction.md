# A trade is the only win-win interaction
Sacrifices 
* to others’s needs, 
* or of others to one’s needs,

aren’t rational approaches to life.
They only create parasitic relationships, which don’t improve the lives of anybody in the long run.
It’s a win-lose solution. And in time it will necessarily become lose-lose.
Trades on the other hand create win-win situations the improve the lives of all people involved.
