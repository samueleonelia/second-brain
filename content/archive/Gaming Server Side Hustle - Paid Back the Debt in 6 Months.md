# Gaming Server Side Hustle: Paid Back the Debt in 6 Months

## The Story

Almost immediately after quitting the bank, an unexpected opportunity appeared. Some gaming kids needed an adult to help set up servers. Yannick handled the technical side. They handled everything else. 50/50 split.

Within 6 months, he made back all the money he owed from the banking lawsuit. From doing "almost nothing." Just by being available when opportunity knocked.

## Lesson for Creators

When you're stuck in the wrong situation, you can't see (or act on) the opportunities around you. Yannick's gaming server deal wasn't something he could have planned. It appeared because he was free, available, and open. The hidden power of quitting something wrong is that it creates space for what's right.
