# The bonuses of the most expensive option
If you keep a tiny price difference between the offer packages, then the bonuses will make most expensive option more desirable.
You don’t need many. One is enough. If it’s the right one. 
How do you choose it?
The right bonus (or set of bonuses) is worth more than the price difference between the two packages you offer on your Yes-Yes Pricing Table.
