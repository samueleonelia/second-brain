# Keyword density
A good keyword density is 0.5-2% for your main keyword. 
Aim to include your keywords 0.5 – 2 times for every 100 words of text. 
For longer articles, a lower rate is acceptable. If your article is 10 000 words, you do not need to mention the keyword 50 – 200 times.
