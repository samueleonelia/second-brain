# POV + Languaging = Messaging
You need:
* a **Point of View (POW)**, meaning *“What do we stand for?”*
* **languaging**, meaning *“How do we powerfully communicate our POV?”*,
* and **messaging** is, *“What should we say?”*

**Frame, Name, and Claim the problem.**
*Example*
Pfizer invented a new disease.
*“Impotence”* was something no man could accept. 
They **reframed** it in a more specific way. 
*“You’re not a man with a lack of agency. You just have a vascular problem: erectile dysfunction. And we have the perfect solution: Viagra!”*
Erectile dysfunction = new **name**
This way they **claim** ownership over that category.
