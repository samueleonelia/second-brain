# The CTA inside an online article
You have 3 options:
* put the CTA in the signature of the article, it’s the less effective way,
* make the CTA as part of an example in your piece (“btw if you want to learn how to create an info product, I have a *(free email course)[link]*),
* put it inside the piece with no specific explanation, associate the link to a relevant phrase.
