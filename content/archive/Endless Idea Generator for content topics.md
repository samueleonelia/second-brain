# Endless Idea Generator for content topics
Follow 3 steps.
1. What kind of content are you writing?
   * Actionable guide
   * Opinion (“Here’s why”)
   * Curated list
   * Story (or Case Study)
   * Motivational piece (“Yes, you can”)
   * Analysis (the numbers)
2. What kind of idea do you want to communicate?
   * Explanation (When/Where/How/What/Why something happens)
   * Habits (to achieve a result)
   * Mistakes (that prevent a result)
   * Lessons (learned achieving a result)
   * Tips (to achieve a result)
   * Stories (that symbolize or explain an important part of the process towards the result)
   * Timely event (relevant for the audience)
3. How are you credible?
   * “I’m an expert on this field, because I did X”
   * “I talked with trusted experts on this field”
   * “I only share my opinion, but it’s the most articulated of all”
