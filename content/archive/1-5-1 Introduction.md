# 1-5-1 Introduction
The role of each sentence:
1. opener,

__
2. clarifies the opener,
3. reinforces your point with a credibility statement,
4. expands from the credibility statement,
5. explains to the readers why you’re telling them what you’re telling them,
6. drives home the point,

__
7.  strong conclusion
