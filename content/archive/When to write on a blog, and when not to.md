# When to write on a blog, and when not to
Write on your website if you already:
1. know what articles people want from you,
2. have defined your category and how to target it,
3. gained the attention of your readers elsewhere or you’re ready to bring them through SEO or ADS.

Examples:
* [AdEspresso](adespresso.com)
* [Kindlepreneur](kindlespreneur.com)

Otherwise write on a social platform to have a faster feedback loop.
