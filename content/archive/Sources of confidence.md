# Sources of confidence
When Jim Simmons was building the Medallion Fund nothing worked straightaway. But he maintained his self-confidence.
Part of it came from his previous success. 
And the rest from the tiny progress along the way. 
To reinforce it he brought in, smart original thinkers. This helped to balance out the moments of doubts.
