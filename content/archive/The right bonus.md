# The right bonus
It’s such if he has one or more of these features:
* helps the use of the product in a particular situation *(ie. Writing course -> bonus “How to write viral Twitter threads”)*
* makes the use of the product easier *(ie. Writing course -> bonus “Template for How-to articles”)*
* it’s personalised *(ie. Writing course -> bonus “1-hour private call”)*;
* it eliminates a related pain  *(ie. Writing course -> bonus “How to find clients as a content writer”)*.
