# Title as a question about the reader’s problem
The simplest formula is: *“Tired of THE PROBLEM?”*
This always works, but you have other options:
* describe the moment in which the problem ruins the life of the reader (*”Do you find yourself in this painful situation due to THE PROBLEM?”*),
* create a comparison between the reader and other people free of that particular problem (*”Why everybody is free from THE PROBLEM, except you?”*),
* use the reader’s fear against him (*”Are you afraid of THE PROBLEM?”*).
