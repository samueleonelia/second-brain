# 1-3-2-1 Introduction
The role of each sentence:
1. opener,

__
2. clarifies the opener,
3. reinforces your point with a credibility statement,
4. rounds out the argument, guiding the reader toward your conclusion,

__
5. strong conclusion,
6. explains why you make such a strong conclusion,

__
7.  what you’re going to tell next to the reader.
