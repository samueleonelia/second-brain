# Setup business experiments
You need to test your business assumptions. This way you understand if your choices are right or wrong.
First of all you have to make a decision. For example: *”I choose to start a blog”*.
After that you put it in practice and evaluate the results, with 1 important caveat.
**Duration and intensity  of the test do count**. If I blog once a month for  a semester I will not acquire reliable data. After such experiment every evaluation will be as random as before its beginning.
But how to know what duration and intensity is necessary for a test?
Every context is different. 
So first of all to set up a valuable experiment **study best practices in your industry and cases similar to yours**. 
After that and also during the phase of test **ask for [[Qualified external opinions]]**.
