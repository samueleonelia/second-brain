# Price justification for consulting services
> “My fee is based on my contribution to the project and represents a dramatic ROI (return of investment) for you and equitable compensation for me” 
> *Alan Weiss, Million Dollar Consulting*

This is how equal partners work together. They ground their business relationship on an equitable quid pro quo.
Hence always consider:
* how much extra profit you provide to the customers,
* and/or how much time you save for them, and its value for them.
