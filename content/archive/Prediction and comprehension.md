# Prediction and comprehension
To predict a result you don’t always need to know the causes behind it.
Ancient people anticipated that the Sun will rise from the east. But they had no knowledge of the cause of that phenomena.
Jim Simmons and his Medallion Fund can predict certain movement in the stock market. But often they don’t know all the causes behind it.
**This “blind” prediction can still be profitable.**
