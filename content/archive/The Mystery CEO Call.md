# The Mystery CEO Call

## The Story

After a job interview that didn't go well, Yannick got a mysterious phone call. The CEO of a different firm somehow knew he was looking. He didn't say how (to avoid placement fees).

The minute Yannick walked in, he knew it was right. They instantly clicked.

This digital marketing agency changed everything. Everyone there had side projects. SEO was fresh, a gold rush. Yannick started a finance website using his banking knowledge, something 99% of people didn't have.

First month: €4 in revenue. Second month: €25. But he kept going. Hours each night, building, tweaking, optimizing. Soon it was hundreds, then thousands of euros monthly. Within two years, his side projects paid more than his actual job.

## Lesson for Creators

Your "useless" past experience is someone else's goldmine. Yannick's banking background seemed like a dead end after the lawsuit. But it gave him domain expertise that 99% of people lacked, enough to build a finance website that outearned his salary. The knowledge you're sitting on from your "previous life" might be exactly what a niche audience is hungry for.
