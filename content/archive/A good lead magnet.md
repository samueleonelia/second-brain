# A good lead magnet
A good lead magnet is fast to read and to apply. That way readers start to trust you for the results you bring them.
If you give them something small that target a common problem, they apply. They get a (small) result. And they trust you more.
The more your subscribers go through the sales flow, the more they will trust you.
Stay away from the idea of a [[Looong lead magnet]]
