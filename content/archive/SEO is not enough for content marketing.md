# SEO is not enough for content marketing
An “ultimate guide” brings visitors, maybe even the right people, but:
* doesn’t spark engagement,
* doesn’t prove real expertise,
* doesn’t add value,
* it’s boring.

Use a contrarian opinion to stand out.
