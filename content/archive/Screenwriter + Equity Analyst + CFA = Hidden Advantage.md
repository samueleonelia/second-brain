# Screenwriter + Equity Analyst + CFA = Hidden Advantage

## The Story

Trung's unusual combination of skills is the real engine behind his content. He's a comedy screenwriter (knows story structure and punchlines), an equity analyst (can read financial statements and understand business models), and a CFA charterholder (deep financial literacy).

He identified a gap: "There was very little content about business and tech that genuinely made people laugh. Most business content is dry; most comedy content lacks depth." Trung sits in the overlap.

His screenwriting background gives him the "show don't tell" instinct. His finance background gives him credible analysis. The combination produces content that educates and entertains at the same time, making it highly shareable.

This gap wouldn't have been visible to a pure writer or a pure analyst. It was only visible from the intersection.

## Lesson for Creators

Your competitive advantage isn't your strongest single skill. It's the intersection of skills that nobody else has. Trung isn't the best screenwriter, the best analyst, or the best meme creator. But he might be the only person who is all three. Find the intersection of your skills and build there. That's where there's no competition.
