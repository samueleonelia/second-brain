# Problem → Result → Solution
This the mental flow behind a customer’s decision. It’s the foundation for every good copy.
**Problem**
* The pain of the customers.
* It grabs their attention.

**Result**
* It’s the opposite of the problem. 
* It represents life without the problem.
* You promise it to the customers to maintain their attention.
* It isn’t your product or service.

**Solution**
* It’s your product or service (or free bonus).
* It’s the bridge between the problem and the result.
