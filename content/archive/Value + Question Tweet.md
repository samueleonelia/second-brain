# Value + Question Tweet
 [Example](https://twitter.com/kjellvdv/status/1372233992880152577?s=21): 
> *Landing page essentials:*
> - *A title that shows a benefit* 
> - *Subtitle that solves possible* objections
> - *CTA that tells you what you get*
>   *Agree, disagree, what else?*

Take some information and write down the 3-5 key points. **It’s better to leave something out.** This way followers will comment and share their own experience. 
**Ways to invite interaction**
* What else?
* Agree, disagree, what else?
* Why? 
* Who else?
