# A method to choose between alternative business priorities
1. Keep a [[Decision journal]]
2. Work mostly (if possible 80% or more) on 1 elements of the business ([[Visitors → Subscribers → Customers]]) at the time.
   1. Choose what works less 
   2. [[Hard decisions are in fact, easy]]
   3. [[Follow your passion]] to choose between equivalent options
3. Reevaluate your priority when you have new relevant information. To get there,  [[Setup business experiments]]
