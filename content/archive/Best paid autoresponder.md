# Best paid autoresponder
The main autoresponders (ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp, CovertKit and MailerLite) all have the features necessary for a creator in terms of:
* automations,
* segmentations,
* integrations.

The main difference is in the cost.
This is the list from the best down. Between parentheses the monthly price for 1.000 subscribers, updated on October 2021:
1. MailerLite (10 $) -> but many automations have to go through Zapier
2. ConvertKit (29 $)
3. ActiveCampaign (70 $)

Mailchimp costs less than ActiveCampaign (51,99 $) but with a limit on the number of emails you can send.
