# Reader Acquisition Flywheel
* Write content in each of your 3 Content Buckets. 
* Use data to decide which specific topic deserves a Pillar Piece. 
* Create a Pillar Piece on your website. 
* Write more about that proven topic in social media, and link to that Pillar Piece when relevant.
