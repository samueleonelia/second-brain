# To make your business solid, do not focus only on the immediate ROI
It is exciting to get great results for your efforts.
Working on low return activities is harder. But [[Growing a business is a continuous balancing act]]. 
If you focus only on the already-working elements of your business, you will  make it less solid in case of a downturn.
