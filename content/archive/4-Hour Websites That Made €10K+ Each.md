# 4-Hour Websites That Made €10K+ Each

## The Story

After proving the finance website model, Yannick got strategic about what he built. He created websites that were tiny but perfectly monetized. Some he spent maybe 4 hours total building, and they each netted him over €10,000 across 5 years.

He did the math: one hour on a client project earned him under €1,000. But that same hour invested in his own sites generated 15x more over time. His 4-hour websites worked out to roughly €2,500/hour. No client pays that.

He scaled the approach to over 100 websites, collectively generating 50,000+ organic monthly visitors. This passive income portfolio became the runway that made the Hypefury bet possible: when he offered to work for free for 2 months, he could afford it because his websites were paying the bills.

## Lesson for Creators

Build assets, not services. An hour spent on client work earns you once. An hour spent on your own project can earn you for years. Yannick's €2,500/hour effective rate from a 4-hour website beats any freelance rate. The question isn't "what can I sell my time for today?" It's "what can I build in 4 hours that will pay me for the next 5 years?"
