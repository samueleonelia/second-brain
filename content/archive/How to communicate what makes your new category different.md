# How to communicate what makes your new category different
You have to elaborate a specific **Point of View**.
Its purpose is to elaborate:
1. what is your new category,
2. and why it is different.

Keep in mind that Specificity Is The Secret.
The more precise you target your readers pains or desires the more you’ll separate yourself from the competition.
