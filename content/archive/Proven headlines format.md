# Proven headlines format
* **Big Numbers** -> *3,000 People Just Filed For Unemployment In This Small Town In Arkansas. Here’s Why.*
* **Dollar Signs**
* **Credible names**
* **“This Just Happened”** -> Good for Trend Jacking
* **The Success Story**
* **Things That Shouldn’t Go Together**
* **For The Industry** -> Specificity Is The Secret
* **The Topic Within The Topic** -> *7 Ways The Real Estate Industry Is Changing (And How You Should Be Investing Your Money).*
* **Question/Answer** -> *Don’t Know How To Track Your Goals? This New App Has The Answer.*
* **X Number** -> 1 Thing or 3 Ways or 5 Lessons…
