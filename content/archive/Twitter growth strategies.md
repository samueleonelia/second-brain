# Twitter growth strategies
* Follow [[Twitter profile best practices]] for the initial set up.
* Don’t overlink inside your tweets (more than 3 links). If possible don’t link at all.
* Quotes are boring and useless
* Write interesting content in a digestible format.
* Share what you’re working on (aka *build in public*)
* Ask good questions. 
  * A good alternative for when you don’t have smart point of view to add to a conversation.
  * It can also be an open question on your roadblocks, 
  * It can be unrelated to any other conversation. You just ask, and make people feel good about themselves.
* Promote others and create interesting connections.
* Learn [[How to retweet]]
* Add value to larger accounts. Summarize their work with unique point of views. This way their followers may have a better chance to see you.
