# Timely vs timeless writing
* Timely is trend-jacking. It gives you a bigger chance of immediate virality.
* Timeless isn’t as viral, but it has a compounding effect. Plus, you can transfer this kind of content from one platform to another.

In online writing, timeless volume wins.
