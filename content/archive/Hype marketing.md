# Hype marketing
It works. Till it doesn’t. And it requires an incredible amount of work in the meantime.
The customers bought from you just for the emotional push of your  [[What is copywriting]]. When the effect vanishes (and it always does), the customers will hate you. They will regret their misplaced trust in you.
The alternative is to bring the buyer through a rational sale process. Reason is slower than emotion. If you convince the customer at that level, you’ll have a loyal one.
