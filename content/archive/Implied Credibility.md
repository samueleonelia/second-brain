# Implied Credibility
1. **How much “Better” or “Worse” your content is** than everyone else’s in your chosen category.
2. A professional **profile picture**.
3. A **bio** that explains clearly in what category your writing is in.
4. High **production quality**.
5. No major **grammar** mistake. And not many of those anyway.
6. Smooth **organization of thoughts**.
7. You use **Specificity** in your content. It’s the Secret.
