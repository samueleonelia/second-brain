# The origin of “positioning”
The beginning is the book The Theory of Monopolistic Competition of [1933](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_differentiation) where Edward Chamberlain present the idea of **product differentiation**.
Then from [1969](https://bit.ly/3F1ouR2) Al Ries and Jack Trout started to popularize the new concept of **positioning**, a proof of *the importance of languaging*. The culmination was the book Positioning of the 1981.
