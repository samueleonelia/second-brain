# Visitors → Subscribers → Customers
Every business online is a variation of this framework:
**Visitors -> Subscribers -> Customers**
You attract **visitors** through ads, content marketing or any other tactic to your website.
There you invite them to become **subscribers** to your [[Mailing list]]. 
And to those that do it, you will propose your products and services. Some of them will buy and will become your **customers**. Yes sometimes people buy even without or before subscribing to a mailing list. It’s a trivial % on the total.
When you work on it remember that:
1. [[There is no strict order to follow in the development of an online business]],
2. [[Growing a business is a continuous balancing act]],
3. [[To make your business solid, do not focus only on the immediate ROI]].
