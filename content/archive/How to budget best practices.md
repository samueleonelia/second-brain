# How to budget best practices
Basic rule: **Give every dollar a job**.
1. Don’t forecast future money. Budget what you already have.
2. Question everything once a year.
3. Be granular in your accounting. This you see what’s necessary or not.
4. Choose your priorities.
5. And try to make more money.
6. Get rid of every personal debt (of course).
