# 1-3-1 + 1-3-1 Introduction
Only for when you **need** a hefty introduction.
Typical use when you have to introduce the main point with a story around another relevant factor.
The role of each sentence:
1. opener,

__
2. clarifies the opener,
3. reinforces your point (credibility statement or expanded description),
4. rounds out the argument, leading to the conclusion

__
5. strong conclusion,

__
6. second opener,

__
7. clarifies the second opener,
8. reinforces your second point (credibility statement or expanded description),
9. rounds out the second argument, leading to the conclusion

__
10. strong conclusion,
