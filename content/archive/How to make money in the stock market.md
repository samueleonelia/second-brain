# How to make money in the stock market
To invest in the stock market along with the 3 [[Rules to invest]], you have only one option. Put your money into index funds. 
* They make you a return in the long term.
* They diversify your investment.
* They keep your costs low if you choose the ones with the lowest fees.

Between index mutual funds and index ETFs, there’re infinite possible choices. But all the suggestions I’ve read are unanimous. Invest in index funds that track the S&P 500.
An S&P 500 index fund with low fees tracks the most important market in the world. And gives you enough diversification. This way you invest in the long term, without stressing out.
