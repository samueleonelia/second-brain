# Envy (or jealousy)
Resentment of, and/or the desire for, objects or qualities possessed by another. 
In the best sense, it refers to the wish of having what another has. 
In the worst sense, it refers to the destructive desire of another person’s values (including virtues of character), virtues, and values which one lacks. (If I can’t have X, then nobody should have it.)
