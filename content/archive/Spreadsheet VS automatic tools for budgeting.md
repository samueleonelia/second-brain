# Spreadsheet VS automatic tools for budgeting
* Using an automatic tool is easier. Less risk of forgetting to track some expense. Most of the operations can be automatise
* Automatic tools expose you to platform risk.
* Spreadsheet can be tailored.
* Writing down your expenses makes your tracking more effective.
* In a spreadsheet you can add notes.
