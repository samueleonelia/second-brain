# Deal breakers in love
The **absence** of these elements, are a deal breaker.
1. The possibility of sharing views on deep topics, such as faith, money, life.
2. The capacity to find a common ground on this topics.
3. The  desire to understand reality the best she can. Not just accept a ready-made answer.
4. A person who put her self-interest first. 
5. Being able to fight well.
