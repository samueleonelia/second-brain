# Optin form and lead’s name
Don’t ask for the lead’s name in the optin form.
Using it in the newsletter create a fake personalization. Plus, it can generate awkward moments if:
* the lead gives you a wrong name,
* you write in a gender specific language.

Even more, it creates unnecessary attrition in the subscription phase.
*”But if they don’t trust you enough  to give you the name, why bother?”*
At this stage they do NOT trust you enough.
That’s why you want them in your mailing list. When they subscribe you have a chance to grow their trust through your newsletters.
