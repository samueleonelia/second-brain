# Thinking through writing
Thinking only inside your brain increases the margin of mistakes.
Write down every idea. 
The un-written ones are useless, because:
* you forget them,
* they require extra mnemonic energy,
* they are not easy to exchange,
* they are not easy to plug in different contexts,
* they lead more often to over or under evaluation of risks.
