# The Humble Brag
You slip in the self-promotional elements like an in-between of your  piece.
* Be casual.
* Go back fast to talk about your reader’s situation.
* Make the self-promotional elements as part of the setting for your piece or story.

Example:
*“I manage the content for a finance website —* **for context, it has +100.000 monthly visitors** *— and one big lesson has been…”*
