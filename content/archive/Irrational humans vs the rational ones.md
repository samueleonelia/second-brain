# Irrational humans vs the rational ones
Using reason is an individual decision. 
Humans can also choose to live without it.
They can:
* imitate in a mechanical way what others do,
* use violence against others and take away from their food and resources.

In both cases the life of these parasites is possible only because someone else used reason in their place.
