# What is copywriting
A writing technique (NO *“art”*) to guide the reader to a specific action.
How can you make the process effective? The basic mental flow to apply is: **[[Problem → Result → Solution]]**.
* A *copywriter* is a writer competent in copywriting.
* A *copy* is a text written with copywriting.
