# Value proposition
It’s a brief statement that highlights the Result: **how you will improve the customer** at the personal or at the business level.
What you do, doesn’t matter.
Only the result you provide, does.
**Bad value propositions:**
* “We provide training in sales skills.”
* “We help to improve your productivity.”
* “We assist in the set up of your funnels.”

**Good value propositions:**
* “We increase the number of your website visitors.”
* “We halve the number of abandoned carts.”
* “We increase the number of customers that buy your upsell offer.”
