# Emotions can be good or evil
Emotions only tell you how you feel about a situation.
If you feel close to your values you’ll have positive emotions.
And viceversa.
In a dangerous situation for your life, you will feel fear. 
It’s a mechanism of protection.
But this means that if you choose irrational values your emotions will stop to work as a protection mechanism. They will instead push you towards your own destruction.
