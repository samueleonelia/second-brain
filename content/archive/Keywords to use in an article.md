# Keywords to use in an article
Choose 5-15 keywords to mention in your article.
* **General main keywords** -> 1-3 words with search volume above 1.000 per month. These keywords will typically take years to rank for due to high competition, and should not be your main target.
* **Specific main keywords.** One level more specific than the general main keywords and your most important keywords. Target 2-5 words with a search volume around 100–1.000 and medium to high competitiveness.
* **Long-tail keywords.** These should be highly niched keywords. 3-6 words and less than 100 searches per month. Ranking on these can help you rank on the more competitive keywords and can take less than a month (if your article is good) and sometimes even overnight (depending on the niche and your domain strength and age).
