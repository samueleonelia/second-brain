# Twitter profile best practices
The profile set up is the starting point of all [[Twitter growth strategies]].  These are the main “rules”.
* Anticipate about what you’ll tweet about.
* Highlight your projects. On one side is a way of promoting them. On the other they give you credibility.
* Follow only real people. No company, or institutional accounts.
* Follow accounts that publish interesting tweets. 
* Unfollow anytime somebody depresses you with his tweets.
