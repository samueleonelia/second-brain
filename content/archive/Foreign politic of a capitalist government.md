# Foreign politic of a capitalist government
Towards other states there’re 3 possible relationships.
1. For those that protect individual rights -> Open diplomatic relationship
2. For those that don’t protect individual rights -> No diplomatic relationship. But citizens can have relationship with it and its residents.
3. For enemies that threat its citizen -> War, if necessary or convenient even a preemptive one.
