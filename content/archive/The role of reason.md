# The role of reason
Reason is the main human’s means of survival.
Our natural instincts don’t tell us:
* how to get food,
* what we can and cannot eat. Or more in general what is good or bad for our life.

We have no automatic information or knowledge. 
For all of this a human needs reason. Same goes for all most advanced decisions and actions that allow us to live and thrive.
