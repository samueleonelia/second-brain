# Three Generations of Programmers: From Punch Cards to AI

## The Story

Boris was born in Odessa, Ukraine. His grandfather was among the first programmers in the USSR, working with punch cards. Boris's mother used to draw on her father's punch cards as a child.

The family emigrated to the United States in 1995. Three generations later, Boris is building AI tools that write code autonomously.

From punch cards to AI-generated code. Three generations of making machines do things.

## Lesson for Creators

Your origin story matters more than you think. Boris's background as a Ukrainian immigrant, raised in a family where programming was in the DNA, gives him a narrative arc that makes his achievements feel inevitable in hindsight. Every creator has a through-line like this. Finding it and telling it honestly gives your audience something to connect with beyond your current work.
