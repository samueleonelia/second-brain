# Online writing is a game
You don’t expect immediate success.
You repeat the process till you improve enough to move to the next level.
1. If you master the system you gain readers’ attention.
2. You’re in competition with all the other creators and their cats.
3. Success isn’t on/off, but a series of incremental improvements.
