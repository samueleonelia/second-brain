# The problem with business priorities
How do you choose where to focus your energy within a creative project or a business?
The easy way is to start somewhere… And get lucky. If results arrive fast you made the right choice. Great!
**Problems arise when you don’t get lucky straight away**. In that case self-doubt takes the driving seat and a bad situation becomes worse.
