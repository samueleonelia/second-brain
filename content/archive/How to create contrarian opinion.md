# How to create contrarian opinion
1. Find a truism in your industry.
2. Define opposite ideas that reflect your beliefs.
3. Search evidence to back your ideas.
4. Prepare your argument.
   * Thesis -> What people believe.
   * Antithesis -> Why is wrong.
   * Synthesis -> New correct view.
5. Create a catchphrase.
