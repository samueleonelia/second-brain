# "Shit I'm Fucked" Live Events

## The Story

Hampton runs a live event format where 10 entrepreneurs tell 5-minute stories of their worst business failures to an audience of 100 founders. The format originated from a Hampton Slack channel.

A NYC event featured someone who raised $100M and blew it in 2 years, a $30M/year company brought down by a lawsuit, and someone with 5 sandwich franchises who went bankrupt.

Sam describes it as "very cathartic to laugh at our miseries and feel less alone." Plans to make it recurring.

## Lesson for Creators

Failure content outperforms success content because it creates connection. Success stories inspire, but failure stories bond. People don't pay $9,500/year to hear how great everyone is. They pay to hear someone say "I raised $100M and lost it all" and realize they're not alone in struggling. If you're building community, make space for vulnerability, not just victories.
