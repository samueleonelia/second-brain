# Mystery + Conspiracy + Unnatural 
The stories that I get hooked the most has these 3 elements:
* mystery, with some kind of detective story,
* conspiracy, an organization trying to manipulate society, a group of people or someone in particular,
* unnatural, an element of magic or sci-fi, it can be real or just part of the conspiracy.

Examples:
* Hellbound,
* 20th Century Boy,
* Billy Bat.
