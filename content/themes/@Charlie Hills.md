# Charlie Hills

Charlie Hills is a LinkedIn creator and AI content strategist who grew from zero to 185,000+ followers in under two years while earning 7x his previous corporate salary. Before LinkedIn, he co-founded a food tech startup called Kitchen X and spent four years quietly using AI tools in hospitality roles. He runs the MarTech AI newsletter (48,000+ subscribers), a LinkedIn ghostwriting agency called Linked Agency, and teaches creators how to use AI without losing their voice.

## Key Patterns

- Every system he teaches (CHEF, 70/20/10, engagement circuit) is one he uses daily on his own account
- He treats AI as prep work, not finished product, always filtering output through the "stranger test"
- Data overrides assumptions in his workflow: he exports analytics monthly and lets the numbers guide decisions
- He builds monetization into content from the start through deliberate funnel architecture
- He survived six months of invisibility by relying on systems instead of motivation
